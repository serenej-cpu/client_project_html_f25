Explore Your Career

Find Your Path

Navigating your career path can be both exciting and challenging. At UMSI, we are dedicated to providing you with the resources and support you need to explore various career options, understand industry trends, and connect with professionals in your field of interest. Whether you're considering internships, full-time positions, or further education, our Career Development Office is here to guide you every step of the way.

Start by exploring our curated resources, attending career workshops, and leveraging our extensive network of alumni and industry partners. Your journey to a fulfilling career begins here!

Discover Student-Led Opportunities

Get Involved

Engaging with student-led organizations and initiatives is a fantastic way to enhance your career exploration journey. These groups offer hands-on experience, networking opportunities, and a chance to develop leadership skills that are highly valued by employers. Whether you're interested in tech, data science, information management, or any other field within the information sector, there's likely a student organization that aligns with your interests.

Participate in events, workshops, and projects organized by these groups to gain practical experience and connect with like-minded peers. Your involvement can open doors to new opportunities and help you.
